The Wayfinders: Character Sheets

 Previous Next

Overview

These are a set of character design sheets created for my 2025 Capstone Senior Project. The project includes a fully-rendered full body reference, six bust sketches: three for different angles, three for different expressions, and prop sketches.
Feel free to click on the images to get a full-size view.

The world is as follows:

The Wayfinders is a story set in a distant future where a combination of global warming and a violation of ethical human research has led to large portions of the world being submerged underwater. In what was once the Pacific, scattered pockets of civilization have been rebuilding for two centuries.

Among them are the city-states of Arkadia and Lāhui. When a group of young people from both lands meet, they must try to work together as they handle disputes between the citizens and seek the mysterious underwater haven known as Kahiki.

Kaimana Leleiohoku (Protagonist)

The youngest descendant of one of the founding families of Lāhui. Charismatic yet carefree, his passions lie in sports and oceanography.

Kyra Kymani (Protagonist)

A commander's daughter and aspiring cartographer hailing from Arkadia. She's a rough-and-tumble girl with boundless optimism, chasing the thrill of adventure.

Jiro Yonamine (Deuteragonist)

The second son of a repair shop owner in Arkadia, with a knack for inventions and photography. He's resourceful and creative, yet also sarcastic and blunt.

Emmalani McNamara (Deuteragonist)

The daughter of two farmers and scholars in Lāhui. With a gentle disposition, she's talented in song and dance, along with a passion for old history and myths.

Sivasa Ulimasao (Deuteragonist)

Having arrived in Lāhui from Atumotu several years ago, she's the hānai daughter of a fisherman. Resilient and protective, she's knowledgeable in celestial navigation methods.
